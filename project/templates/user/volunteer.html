WISDOM ACROSS GENERATIONS

"Stories are the bridge between the past and the future."

Meet our Guardian Elders. Ranging from 65 to 85 years young, this dedicated group joined "Our Story Our Voice" with a singular mission: to ensure that the oral histories of their communities serve as lessons for the next generation.

Together, they spearhead our Heritage Mentorship Program, teaching young volunteers the delicate art of listening, patience, and how to conduct respectful interviews that bridge the generational gap.
