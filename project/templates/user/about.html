About Us

Our Story Our Voice

Shining a spotlight on the rich tapestry of Canada's ethno-cultural communities.

Empower. Amplify. Celebrate.

"Our Story Our Voice" is a platform that not only empowers individuals but also enriches the broader societal narrative. By sharing stories, amplifying voices, and celebrating diverse perspectives, we foster a deeper understanding of who we are as a nation.

Through storytelling, media, and various forms of artistic expression, our goal is to:

  • Empower individuals and communities to share their unique experiences.
  • Amplify marginalized voices and promote representation.
  • Celebrate the diversity that makes Canada so strong.
  • Shape the narrative of ethno-cultural communities, challenging stereotypes.
  • Foster a sense of belonging and connection.
  • Contribute to a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Our Mission

To empower, amplify, and celebrate the diverse voices of ethno-cultural communities in Canada through storytelling, media, and cultural expression—shaping narratives that reflect our lived experiences, heritage, and contributions to society.

Our Vision

A Canada where every ethno-cultural community is seen, heard, and valued—where our stories shape the national narrative, foster unity in diversity, and inspire future generations with pride, resilience, and creativity.

Our Mandates

01

Storytelling & Media

  • Produce and promote authentic stories through video, audio, and photo.
  • Create platforms for community members to share lived experiences.
02

Visibility & Representation

  • Showcase achievements of ethno-cultural organizations.
  • Challenge stereotypes through inclusive narratives.
03

Community Empowerment

  • Provide training and mentorship to tell stories.
  • Support youth and emerging creators in media skills.
04

Cultural Preservation

  • Document cultural practices, languages, and histories.
  • Encourage fusion of traditional and contemporary expressions.
05

Advocacy & Collaboration

  • Partner with organizations to influence policy.
  • Advocate for inclusive media representation.
06

Public Engagement

  • Host festivals, exhibitions, and intercultural dialogues.
  • Develop educational content highlighting Canada’s mosaic.
Leadership

Mr Dax

Founder & Executive Director

"I founded Our Story Our Voice driven by the belief that the true strength of Canada lies in the stories that often go unheard. Having navigated my own journey of cultural identity, I realized the transformative power of seeing yourself represented in the media."

Dax brings over 15 years of experience in community advocacy and media production. His vision is to build a lasting platform where every ethno-cultural community feels the pride of belonging and the power of their own narrative.
