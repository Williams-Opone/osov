1 Introduction

Welcome to Our Story Our Voice. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy governs the privacy policies and practices of our website and application. Please read our privacy policy carefully as it will help you make informed decisions about sharing your personal information with us.

2 Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website.

A. Personal Information

  • • Identity: Names, usernames.
  • • Contact: Email addresses, phone numbers.
  • • Auth Data: Email & picture via Google OAuth.

B. Payment Data

If you use our "Donate" feature, we collect necessary processing data.

Note: We do NOT store credit card details.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To facilitate account creation and logon process (including Google OAuth).
  • Donations: To manage your contributions and provide receipts.
  • Stories & Testimonials: With your consent, we may post your story along with your name.
  • Communication: To send administrative information about terms, policies, or service updates.

4 Sharing Your Information

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, or to fulfill business obligations.

Service Providers We share data with vendors who perform services for us (e.g., Google for auth, Stripe for payments).
Legal Obligations We may disclose info where legally required to comply with applicable law or judicial proceedings.

5 Third-Party Logins (Google)

Our website allows you to register and log in using your Google account. If you choose to do this, we receive certain profile information (Name, Email, Profile Picture).

We do not control other uses of your personal information by Google. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy.

6 Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures (such as SSL encryption) to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

7. Your Privacy Rights (NDPR)

We are committed to maintaining the accuracy, confidentiality, and security of your personally identifiable information. Our privacy policy is governed by applicable Canadian and International privacy laws.

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent for data use.
  • File a complaint regarding privacy practices.

8 Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date.
