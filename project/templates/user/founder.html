From the Niger Delta to the World

Engr. Oyibo's formative years were spent in Sapele, a vibrant commercial hub whose multicultural atmosphere profoundly influenced his worldview. Growing up in the Niger Delta region, he developed the resilience, cultural pride, and entrepreneurial spirit that would define his journey.

His academic excellence emerged early at Urhobo College, Warri, where he held an impressive portfolio of leadership positions including Assistant Senior Prefect and President of the Dramatic, Science, and Literary & Debating Clubs.
