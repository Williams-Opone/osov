Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you reach out.

How do I submit a story?

We are currently accepting story pitches via the contact form above. Select "Pitch a Story Idea" and give us a brief summary. Our editors review pitches weekly.

Are your events free?

Most of our community workshops are free or "pay-what-you-can." Larger galas or fundraising events may require purchased tickets.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We often need help with social media, translation, and digital content creation. Mention "Volunteering" in your message.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, OSOV is a registered non-profit. We issue official tax receipts for all donations over $20.
