Community Program

The EICP is designed to provide school support, mentorship, and intercultural training for immigrant youth (Grades 7-12) and displaced individuals.

Learn About EICP

Moving to a new country shouldn't mean losing your voice.

For thousands of immigrant youth in Calgary, the reality is isolation. Language barriers, cultural shock, and a lack of mentorship create a gap that talent often falls through.

Isolation • Barriers • Silence

We build the bridge to belonging.

"Our Story Our Voice" provides the platform, equipment, and mentorship needed to turn isolation into connection. We don't just teach storytelling; we build leaders.
